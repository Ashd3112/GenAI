AURA PREMIER AUTO POLICY - CERTIFICATE OF INSURANCE
Policy Number: POL-99482-01
Policyholder: Standard Certificate
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

SECTION 1: COVERAGE & LIMITS
1.1 Collision Coverage: Aura will pay for direct, sudden, and accidental loss or damage to the covered vehicle subject to a $500 deductible. Maximum limit per occurrence: $50,000.
1.2 Comprehensive Coverage: Covers non-collision damage including fire, theft, wind, vandalism, and animal impact. Deductible: $250.
1.3 Medical Payments: Limit of $5,000 per passenger for medical treatment incurred within one year of the incident.

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS
2.1 Commercial Use Exclusions: Any incident occurring while the vehicle is used for commercial services, ridesharing (e.g. Uber/Lyft), delivery operations, or commercial racing is strictly EXCLUDED from coverage.
2.2 Non-Standard Custom Enhancements: Damages to custom wheels, aftermarket performance exhausts, custom audio stereos, or non-factory paint jobs are EXCLUDED from standard collision coverage unless a Special Equipment Endorsement is active.
2.3 Unlawful Acts: Damages arising from incidents where the operator is operating under the influence of illegal substances or driving without a valid license will result in an immediate DENIAL of the claim.
